APOGEE STRENGTHS

$APGE is a deflationary token that also redistributes coins to holders. 5% of each transaction is split into 2.5% which is burned and 2.5% which is deposited to lucky holders. With each transaction, the amount of $APGE shrinks and the amount of tokens in each wallet grows.

SHRINKING SUPPLY

There were only 10 million $APGE tokens minted with zero being minted in the future. This means that the supply will only go down, increasing value for all holders. This gives investors a strong reason to hodl.
